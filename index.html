About me

Hello world! I'm Ernesto Garcia an animator from Tepic Nayarit that has been living in Monterrey for the past 8 years I graduted from a design school near la Huasteca called "CEDIM" about 3 years now, since then I've been working on several projects from making animated traning videos for companies abroad to working on an animated movie here in San Pedro where I was the set supervisor, I had to deliver the scenes to the lighting deparment in the final stage of modeling, I was also in charge of a group of modelers who were in charge of replacing the low poly scenes with those of high poly, sadly the project ran out of money and I had to leave.

Now I'm working as a community manager in a company here in Monterrey and also as a freelancer for another company doing some 3d modeling and 3d motion graphics, and now that my schedule is a little loose I figured out that i have the time to learn something new, that is why im planning on learn the prestigious art of web development.

So another chapter of my boring life starts here, sit tight cuz we are about to go on board on a challenging journey, stay tune and good night.
